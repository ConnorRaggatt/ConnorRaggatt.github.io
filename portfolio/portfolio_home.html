University Projects

METR2800 - Infra-red tracking robot "Satellite"
Year 2 Semester 2, 2019
A robot suspended by a wire that would use a flywheel to rotate and locate infra-red light sources on targets and then fire back at them.
ENGG1200 - 3D Printed Glider
Year 1 Semester 2, 2018
A glider designed to be launched off a rail. It was built using mostly 3D printed parts and performed relatively well.
ENGG1100 - Search and Rescue Robot
Year 1 Semester 1, 2018
A robot that would search a table for magnetically charged plates and dispense a marker on them.
